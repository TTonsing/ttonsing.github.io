***
**Dingdiang:** (adj. )
- something which is feeble  
  --1. zawi    
  --2. Pan   
  --3. zang   
  --4. Kikhai dingdiang  
  
**Diangdiang:** (adj. )
- something which is late, slow & feeble  
  --1. zekai    
  --2. khonung   
  --3. kiplou   
  --4. Tawm Tawm, zuan zuan
  --5. Taileh hehu
